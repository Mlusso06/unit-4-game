Gemstone Collector Game

Instructions: You will be given a random number at the start of the game. Ther are four gemstone below to collect. Each gemstone has a specific amount of points, and each time you click on the gemstone, you accumulate points. "Guess the points to achieve the correct score" You win a game by matching the correct amount of gemstones with the random number given to you at the beginning of the game. The value of each gemstone is hidden. At the beginning of each game, the values for the gemstone will change.
